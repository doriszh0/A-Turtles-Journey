Combat Climate Change

  • Reuse, reduce, and recycle
  • Travel as sustainably as you can
  • Buy local food
  • Compost


    Minimize Marine Pollution

  • Clean up litter/Throw waste in their proper bins Litter always has a way of ending up in the ocean
  • Attend beach cleanups! --> Coastal Cleanups

    Respect the Wildlife

  • Turn off your lights at night if you live in a coastal area
  • Do NOT pick up wild animals and disturb them from their natural habitat


    Knowledge is Power

  • Spread awareness, in fact share this website with others, so they can know more about the issue
  • Learn More... By increasing your knowledge, you can be better suited to educate others and also make simple changes in your daily life that could have a huge impact


    Money: Simple but Effective

  • Any donation to a reputable organization, regardless of the amount, can help the cause --> Donate for a Sea Turtle
  • Start a fundraiser for wildlife conservation



    Don't just be a bystander to something that very well threatens our entire planet!
    Be willing to learn more and take action in order to Save Our Earth!
